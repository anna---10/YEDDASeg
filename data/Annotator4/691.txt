TITLE: Women should serve in the army like men

CATEGORY: Society

Why you think that only military women can face stress, depression, anxiety and nerves. It is not matter what is your job , every person can suffer from this kind of moral diseases, but not only military women if we think nowadays almost every third person is suffer from stress or even from depression. This reason cannot be barrier for women to serve in army.
