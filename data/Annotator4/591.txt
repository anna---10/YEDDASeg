TITLE: People should be free to avoid getting involved homosexual marriage

CATEGORY: Politics

If a person doesn't want to give a marriage certificate for a gay couple, the person should be free to do so. If a person doesn't want to plan a wedding for a gay couple, the person should be free to do so.
